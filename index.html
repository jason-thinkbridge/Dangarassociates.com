About



Dangar Associates

Dangar Associates is a full-service income tax and accounting firm specializing in small business solutions. We understand that the main focus of the small business owner is running their business. Unfortunately, that means that administrative tasks such as proper bookkeeping or keeping up with required governmental documentation sometimes take a backseat to the day-to-day operations of the business.

Dangar Associates puts Enrolled Agents, Accredited Tax Advisors and Accredited Tax Preparers to work on behalf of you and your business. We not only prepare individual returns but also complex business income tax returns, represent clients before the Internal Revenue Service for corporate and individual tax audits, tax liens, payroll tax delinquencies, and the Offer and Compromise tax reduction program.

When you partner with Dangar Associates, you gain a CFO for your company. Whether your business is in need of full-service bookkeeping from invoicing to applying payments and reconciling bank accounts, or if you are simply looking for help with quarterly payroll filings or tax advice, our flexible menu of services allows us to create a solution for your business that fits both your needs and your budget.

One of the most important aspects of owning a business is knowing when to delegate tasks so that your time can be spent growing your company. A partnership with Dangar Associates gives you the freedom to expand your business and the knowledge that a team of local professionals are keeping track of your bottom line.

Dangar Associates Privacy Policy

We do not disclose any nonpublic personal information about our clients or former clients to anyone. We collect nonpublic personal information about you from the following sources:  Information we receive from you on applications or other forms;  Information about your transactions with us, our affiliates, or others;  Information we receive from a consumer reporting agency;  Information we receive from interviews regarding client’s tax situations;  Information we receive on applications, organizers or by other means, such as client names, addresses, telephone numbers, Social Security Numbers, dependents, income and other tax-related data;  Information from tax-related documents that we require from clients in order to process their tax returns (Form W-2, 1099R, 1099-INT and 1099-DIV, and stock transactions, etc) We do not disclose any nonpublic personal information about our clients or former clients to anyone, except as permitted by law. We restrict access to nonpublic personal information about you to those employees who need to know that information to provide products or services to you.  We maintain physical, electronic, and procedural safeguards that comply with federal regulations to guard your nonpublic personal information. We do not release or discuss any information to any 3rd party about you either by phone, mail, fax, email or in person. This includes any information requested in regard to loan purposes. We will not fax you any information to telephone numbers not previously received from you. We provide copies of all information to you at the time of services being paid and rendered. Any additional copies will incur a fee for copies and handling. The copies or information requested may be picked up at our office, only by those whose names appear on the information, or we will mail those copies to last addresses found on record per information given by you. If your address should change during the year, please timely mail us a signed change of address card. Any IRS or State representation work will supersede this privacy policy by a signed power of attorney (Form 2848) in file. If you have any questions about our Privacy Policy, please contact us. Greg Dangar

EA, ATA, ATP, IAR

Owner

Tax Preparation Services

Greg began his income tax career in 1985 while working at a firm in Marietta, GA. After working there for almost eight years, an opportunity opened up in 1992 for him to begin Dangar Associates. He is a graduate of Kennesaw State University with a BBA in accounting and am enrolled to practice before the IRS (EA). He is also an Accredited Tax Preparer (ATP), Accredited Tax Advisor (ATA), and a Certified Tax Professional (CTP). He is a NTPI Fellow® graduate of National Tax Practice Institute (NTPI) earning a distinction as one of the nation’s top tax practitioners. He has twice served as the president of the Georgia Chapter of the National Association of Tax Professionals (NATP) and also have served two terms as a National Board Director for NATP. Frequently, He teaches tax classes for various tax associations on Federal, State and Local tax issues. Lastly, He has passed multiple insurance and securities exams and hold a distinction as an Investment Advisor Representative to offer assistance with retirement plans, rollovers and etc.
